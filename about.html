About Us

Setting the benchmark in gold mining and development around the globe.

Our Mission & Vision

A Legacy of Excellence

Our mission is to create value using our proven “self-perform” approach to acquire, build, and operate precious metal mining operations while focusing on our employees' and host communities' health and safety.

Our goal is to build a leading intermediate gold producer. Gold Mining Experts is recognized as an award recipient and is listed on the TSX. We aim to leverage strong access to capital and proven development expertise.

The Story

Execution & Strategy

The Gold Mining Experts story embodies a commitment to delivering large-scale projects internally rather than relying on the conventional approach of outsourcing to EPCM contractors. After decades of project execution success—delivering projects at or below budget, and on schedule—our approach generates substantial value for shareholders.

We are anchored by a robust asset base of producing gold mines in South America and advanced exploration projects worldwide, operating exclusively in mining friendly jurisdictions.

Creating lasting value through expert mining consultancy, responsible practices, community engagement, and environmental stewardship.
