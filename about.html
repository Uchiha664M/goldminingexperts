About Us

Setting the benchmark in gold mining development through our proven "self-perform" approach to project execution.

Our Mission & Vision

Creating Value Through Proven Mine Development

Our mission is to create value using our proven "self-perform" approach to acquire, build, and operate precious metal mining operations while focusing on our employees' and host communities' health and safety.

Our goal is to build a leading intermediate gold producer. Gold Mining Experts is recognized as an award recipient and listed on major exchanges. We aim to leverage strong access to capital and proven development expertise to deliver consistent value for our shareholders.

The Story

A Legacy of Execution Excellence

The Gold Mining Experts story embodies a commitment to delivering large-scale projects internally rather than relying on the conventional approach of outsourcing to EPCM contractors. After decades of project execution success—delivering projects at or below budget, and on schedule—our approach generates substantial value for shareholders.

With a combined construction cost of over US$2 billion, our team has delivered 100% of its projects at or below budget, and on schedule. Gold Mining Experts was created to acquire direct ownership of projects and capitalize on the value uplift that successful mine development offers.

With strong access to capital via a supportive shareholder base coupled with proven development expertise, we are well positioned to grow the company into the next mid-tier precious metal producer. We are anchored by a robust asset base of producing gold mines and advanced exploration projects across the Americas, operating exclusively in mining friendly jurisdictions.
