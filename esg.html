Our Commitment

Sustainable Mining Practices

Gold Mining Experts remains committed in our dedication to sustainability and environmental responsibility. We recognize that our decisions and activities have a direct impact on a broad group of stakeholders, including employees, investors, local communities, and regulators.

We follow a governance model based on transparency and socio-environmental responsibility, ensuring that our processes and strategies are aligned with recognized industry practices. Sustainable mining is only achievable through active engagement with local communities and the creation of shared value.

Environmental

100% GHG emissions reported and managed. Completed 18 hectares of reforestation. 73.9% of solid waste recycled across operations.

Social

Zero Harm maintained across operations. 67% local labor sourced. 15.2% women in workforce, up from 11% in prior year. 2,300+ students in community programs.

Governance

100% supplier development actions implemented. 52% compliance with International Cyanide Management Code. Three new corporate ESG policies adopted.
